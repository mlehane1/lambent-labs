Hot take: Low-code platforms aren't dead. They're about to become AI's best friend.

Everyone's saying "AI kills low-code." I think the opposite is true.

When AI generates React code, it has to navigate hundreds of patterns, conventions, and folder structures. When it works with a model-driven platform like Mendix, the rules are fixed. The structure is defined. The guardrails are built in.

For regulated industries and enterprises that need governance, that matters more than flexibility.
